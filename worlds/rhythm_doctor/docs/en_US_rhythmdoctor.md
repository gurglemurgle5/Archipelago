# Rhythm Doctor

## Where is the options page?

The [player options page for this game](../player-options) contains all the options you need to configure and export a config file.

## What does randomization do to this game?

Levels are locked behind their respective items, and clearing levels will not unlock other levels or its night shift. Wards are locked behind a key item, and night shift levels are considered independent from its day shift level.
